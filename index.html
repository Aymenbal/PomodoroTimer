How the Pomodoro Technique Works

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method proven to increase productivity and reduce burnout. By breaking your workflow into focused intervals (typically 25 or 50 minutes), followed by short restorative breaks, you train your brain to maintain a state of flow.

  • Step 1: Select a single task to focus on.
  • Step 2: Set the timer for a 25-minute or 50-minute block.
  • Step 3: Work without interruptions until the alarm chimes.
  • Step 4: Take a 5 or 10-minute break to stretch and reset.
